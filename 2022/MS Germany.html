Masters in Bioinformatics in Germany Eligibility

For admittance to the top Masters in bioinformatics in Germany eligibility criteria need to be fulfilled:

The applicant must hold a university degree (bachelor’s or equivalent), either from Germany or an equivalent foreign degree, from a six-semester university course that certifies them to practice a profession.

Either one (1) can get one’s university degree in an academic institution where English is the language of instruction, or (2) one is required to demonstrate English language abilities at level B2 of CEFR.

At least 25 credits in computer science modules are required. At least ten credits must be in courses where one has learned an essential programming language, like Java, C/C, or Python, and at least ten credits must be in the domain of algorithms.

At least 25 credits in mathematical courses are required. At least ten credits in the domain of linear algebra or analytics and at least ten credits in the domain of statistics.

The applicant must have at least 25 credits in these subjects. At least ten of these credits must be in molecular biology, biochemistry, and genetics.
